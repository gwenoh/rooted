Grown in the US

All our plants are grown locally in greenhouses around the country, free of chemical sprays and pesticides. When you order from Rooted, you know exactly where your plant is coming from and who helped to grow your plant.

Supporting Local Farmers

As part of our mission to protect the environment, we donate 10% of every purchase Farm Aid, a charity supporting local farming communities, helping to keep family farmers on the land.
